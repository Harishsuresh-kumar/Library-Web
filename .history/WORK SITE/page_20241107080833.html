SPIDER MAN

Author: Team Publications

Rent: Rs.150

Rating: ★★★★☆

A Hero in the Shadows.


In the heart of New York City, young Peter Parker was juggling his life as a high school student with his secret role as Spider-Man. One crisp autumn evening, while swinging through the city’s skyline, his spider-sense tingled—a sign of danger nearby.

He landed on a rooftop and peered down. A group of masked robbers was breaking into a jewelry store. Without hesitation, Peter leaped into action, launching a web to stop the nearest thug. “Didn’t your mother ever tell you stealing isn’t nice?” he quipped, dodging a punch and webbing up another assailant.

Just as he was finishing up, the leader of the gang pulled out a strange device, a high-tech gadget that emitted a powerful pulse, pushing Spider-Man backward. Shaken, Peter realized this was no ordinary heist. The gang was after more than jewels—they wanted tech from Oscorp, and the device was meant to break into the company's top-secret lab.

Summoning his strength, Peter devised a plan. He created a distraction by setting off a fire alarm nearby, forcing the thieves to split up. In the confusion, Spider-Man stealthily took them down one by one until only the leader was left. But this time, he took a different approach. Using his wits, Peter tricked the leader into triggering his own device, immobilizing him in a web of electromagnetic waves.

When the police arrived, they found the thieves wrapped up neatly in webs, with Spider-Man long gone. Peter slipped back into his civilian life just in time to finish his homework, a hero in secret, once again saving the city without anyone knowing. And though he faced challenges, Peter Parker knew that, with great power, came great responsibility.
